LolPredictor is an app that predicts the potential result of an Ultra Rapid Fire game. By using the most advanced algorythms and technologies in data mining and machine learning, LolPredictor helps the user to craft the ideal team to success in the most popular League of Legends game mode. To do so, the developers of LolPredictor have carefuly analized over 500,000 Ultra Rapid Fire games, all of them with a huge variety of champions compositions, with the aim of providing this unique tool its extreme accuracy.
